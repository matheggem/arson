ARSON — image slots
===================

These are the files the site references, in scroll order:

  arson-wordmark.png   →  Hand-painted ARSON wordmark (red on black, used
                          as the hero title). PNG with transparent or black
                          background both work.

  jonah-01.jpg         →  First image after the hero (currently the new
                          double-exposed face shot).
  jonah-02.jpg         →  Cello + hands + feet.
  jonah-03.jpg         →  Dancers in white (indoor / double-exposed).
  jonah-05.jpg         →  Hands and cloth (placed after the alchemical
                          quote, before the final image).
  jonah-04.jpg         →  Cloth + figures, outdoors at night (final image
                          before the menu).

If you swap any of these out, keep the same filename and the site picks
it up automatically. To re-order them across the page, edit the data-src=""
attributes on the <div class="img-frame"> elements in index.html.

Future drop-in slot (already wired in JS):

  loop.mp4             →  Hypnotic video loop. Drop it in, then uncomment
                          the <video> block in section 6 (search index.html
                          for "VIDEO LOOP").
